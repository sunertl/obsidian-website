## Recipe

- Water: 400g at 96°C / 205°F
- Coffee: 25g
- Grind: 4 Fellow Ode Gen 2
- Brew:
	- 0:00 Bloom to 50g
	- 0:30 Heavy Center pour to 200g
	- 1:30 Spiral pour to 300g
	- 2:00 Spiral pour to 400g
	- ~3:10 Drain

## Cup Profile

- Mixed berries
- Molasses
- Plum
- 60 % dark chocolate
