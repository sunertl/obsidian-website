## Recipe

- Water: 400g at 94°C / 202°F
- Coffee: 25g
- Grind: 3 Fellow Ode Gen 2
- Brew:
	- 0:00 Bloom to 50g
	- 0:30 Heavy Center pour to 200g
	- 1:30 Spiral pour to 300g
	- 2:00 Spiral pour to 400g
	- ~2:50 Drain

## Cup Profile

- Mixed berries
- Cacao nib
- Sorghum
- Honeydew melon
